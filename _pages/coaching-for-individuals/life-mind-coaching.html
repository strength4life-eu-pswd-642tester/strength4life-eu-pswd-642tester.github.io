Life & Mind Coaching

Live a balanced life – feel good

Enhance your life skills and knowledge through a strong life focus. Through coaching, retreats and mentoring, I help you become confident, strong and balanced.

Choose Your Coaching Path

Focus 4 Life

11 Days — It all begins with a 1:1 session (face to face or online). Together we explore the 4 Pillars, reflect, and create plans to enhance your life.

Strength Plus

11 Weeks — Ongoing support to embed small but significant habits. After the initial 11 hours of focus, daily support helps you create sustainable new routines.

Full Strength

11 Months — Full immersion into life skills, with travel experiences and coaching support to embed lifelong healthy habits and balance.

Why It Matters

You can make a strong impact on your future — your life. Strength4Life coaching is built around the Body • Mind • Heart • Spirit, a realistic and holistic approach to balance.

Everyone is on a unique life journey. I’ll help you build your personal toolkit of skills and strategies for lasting change.
