Travel4Life

A change is as good as a rest. Sometimes what you need isn’t more work — it’s a reset. Whether you’re looking for a break, a “mental health day,” a weekend escape, or simply quality time in nature, Travel4Life creates space for you.

I’ll tailor your Travel4Life experience to your needs and interests. Along the way, we’ll talk openly about where you are, why things are the way they are, and plan for a stronger, brighter future together.


What to Expect

A Travel4Life experience can be whatever you need it to be — a one-day break, a mystery trip, or a weekend retreat. Whether solo, with a partner, or in a small group, you’ll return feeling refreshed, clear, and energised.

  • Be FUN
  • Feel STRONGER
  • More CONFIDENT
  • Positive THINKING
  • Good RELATIONSHIPS


Example Travel4Life Day Out

  • Fly Launceston to Wynyard (Burnie) Airport — just 30 minutes on a 15-seater Metroliner
  • Breakfast in Wynyard & lunch at beautiful Boat Harbour or in the hinterlands
  • Visit Table Cape Tulip Farm (September–October)
  • Personal and professional development time, team-building, or 1:1 sessions


Travel4Life for Businesses

Travel4Life isn’t only for individuals. It’s also a powerful tool for businesses and organisations. Retreats, incentives, and team days provide employees with recognition and balance, boosting productivity and strengthening workplace culture.

With opportunities for team building, personal development, and reflection throughout the day, Travel4Life combines fun and growth to ensure your employees can be the best they can be.
