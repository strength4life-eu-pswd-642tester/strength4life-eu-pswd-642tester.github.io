About Me

Strength4Life encourages and enables you to be the best you can be. My solutions are tailored to your needs — whether you’re an individual looking for balance or an organisation aiming to strengthen your people.

From my own journey, I know how important true balance is. It’s more than work-life balance — it’s about a holistic, strong approach to every part of life. The Strength4Life method not only impacts your wellbeing directly, it also has a ripple effect: on your family, your community, and your organisation.

Why Balance Matters

Around 1 in 5 adults struggle with stress, anxiety or depression — and many face related health issues such as obesity or chronic illness. I’ve seen the toll this takes, and I’ve also seen the power of strong individuals to support and inspire those around them.

A good, healthy balance in life strengthens both your body and your mind. It helps prevent health issues, builds resilience, and enhances your personal and professional life. In today’s changing world, when uncertainty is the new normal, this has never been more important.

“I’ve learnt to have faith in my own abilities and to be more confident to ‘try’! I’ve also learned more about dealing with difficult situations through resilience and personal strength.”

How Strength4Life is Different

My approach is real and authentic. I don’t believe in one-size-fits-all coaching — instead, I work with you through a down-to-earth method that focuses on awareness, reflection, planning and taking action.

Closely aligned with Maslow’s Hierarchy of Human Needs, I support you in forming new habits that lead to small but significant enhancements in your life quality. And I don’t just stop at the session — I follow up and follow through, to make sure the tools we put in place really last.

“Informative and very helpful… how body, mind, heart and spirit relates to the daily life we live in.”

My Story

I grew up on a tiny island in the middle of the ocean — and at 17, I set out to see the world. Fifty years later, I’m still on that journey. I’ve lived in Australia, the USA, the Middle East and Europe, and I’ve now made Germany my home.

Along the way, I’ve worked with people from more than 100 nationalities: in airlines, hospitality, learning & development, and academia. What connects all of it is simple: I love working with people.

My career started in international travel with Air Canada, Qantas and KLM, followed by hospitality management roles with Westin Hotels in Australia and Europe. Later, I worked with the Police Services for the Sydney Olympic Games, and with the Learning & Development team at the Burj Al Arab and Jumeirah Group.

I’ve also trained and lectured at the University of Tasmania and the University of Sydney, bringing my passion for developing others into education. Since 2015, I’ve been the face of Strength4Life — combining all of these experiences into one mission: enabling people to live a balanced, strong, and fulfilling life.

Don’t be surprised if I turn up to a meeting on my motorbike, wearing my leather jacket. That’s me. I love travel, people, food, whisky — and of course, motorbikes.
